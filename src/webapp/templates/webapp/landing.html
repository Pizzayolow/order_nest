Designed and created by a young team of developers, for small companies

OrderNest is a website designed to provide small businesses with a tool for tracking and managing orders. Open-source, it's also versatile, clear, fast, tablet-oriented and easy to access.

Features

We have implemented several features in this project, including:

  • A very simple and readable map view
  • Customer and order creation
  • Adding drawings, photos, and products

Why OrderNest ?

We're two young developers, and we wanted to do a project together to overcome a recurring problem when you start your own company and don't have a lot of resources.

We're in the year 2023, so to see someone still writing down customer information, taking notes or taking orders on a piece of paper seemed outdated to us.
